What is Colour Blindness?

The American Academy of Ophthalmology definition of colour blindness is "Most commonly, color blindness (also known as color deficiency) happens when someone cannot distinguish between certain colors, usually between greens and reds, and occasionally blues. In the retina (the light-sensitive tissue lining the back of the eye), there are two types of cells that detect light: rods and cones"
